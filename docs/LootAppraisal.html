Loot Appraisal

The Loot Appraisal tool is a direct replacement for EvePraisal. It is a simple copy and paste loot on the left, click appraise in the middle and the value of the items is loaded on the right.
You can copy and paste the contents of any item window in any satus and the parser *should* be able to parse it. There may still be some item formats that I haven't handled since every time you copy and paste from a different window in Eve, the format is slightly different.
